Take You There


Come back
In my dreams, your presence lingers, a haunting refrain
A bittersweet reminder of what we could not sustain
My mind, a canvas, painted with memories of you
A masterpiece of moments, both sweet and true
Come back, and let us weave a new shade
One threaded with laughter, tears, and ecstasy
Perhaps, this time, our love will find its way
Through the complex of heartache, to a brighter day
Your departure left an ache, a hollowed-out space
A silence that screams louder than any embrace
Why did you leave? The question echoes through my mind
A mystery that only time and truth can unwind
Come back, and let us rewrite our story’s end
One that concludes with joy, not sorrow’s trend
My heart, a vessel, yearns to be filled anew
With the love we shared, and the dreams we once pursued
Please come back, and let our love shine bright
A beacon in the darkness, guiding us through the night
